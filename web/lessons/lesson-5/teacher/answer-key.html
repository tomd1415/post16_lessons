Activity guidance

  • Four 4's: encourage diverse expressions (sqrt, parentheses, division). Check targets 1–9 are covered.
  • Evaluate Expressions: sample finals — (3+4)*2-5 → 9; 12/3+4*2 → 12; 4*4-4**2 → 0.
  • Fix suggestions — 3 + 4 * 2 to 14: (3+4)*2; 16 / 4 + 4 to 2: 16 / (4+4); 8 - 4 * 2 ** 2 to 0: (8-4*2)**2/4.
  • Look for correct step tracing and saved runner output as evidence.
