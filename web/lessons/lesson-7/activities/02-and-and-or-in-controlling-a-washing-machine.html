Teacher guidance (answer prompts)

  • Quick check 1: both conditions are true
  • Quick check 2: at least one condition is true
  • Quick check 3: Flips it
  • Learning objective focus: Reinforce understanding of OR and AND Boolean
  • Step 2 ideas: AND = both signals; OR = either signal; NOT = block when condition present
  • Outputs to expect: heater, motor, pump, inlet valve, door lock (on/off states)
  • Inputs to expect: water level, temperature, door closed, cycle stage
  • Safety logic examples: heater only with water; lock door if spinning OR water present; pump when spin AND water present
  • Troubleshooting: contact manufacturer if (seal leak OR motor failure) AND under warranty; reset only if door closed AND water drained
