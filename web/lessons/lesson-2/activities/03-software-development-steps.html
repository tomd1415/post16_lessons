Step 4: Reflection

Teacher guidance (answer prompts)

  • Quick checks: (1) Analysis, (2) During/after programming, (3) Improvements after release.
  • Ordering: Analysis → Design → Programming → Testing → Enhancement.
  • Misconceptions: “Testing happens only at the end”; “Enhancement is optional”; “Design = writing code”.
  • Differentiation: give a printed strip-set of phases to physically order; stretch asks for risks (e.g., incorrect logic, missing requirements).
