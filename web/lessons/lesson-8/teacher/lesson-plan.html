Lesson plan

Overview

The final two data types, list and tuple, are introduced in this lesson. Students compare mutability, practice slicing, and manipulate collections to solve small tasks.

They reverse lists, then build a toolkit that packs tuples, mutates lists, and captures errors from attempting to change tuples.

Learning objectives

  • Understand aggregate data types
  • Use aggregate data in a program like: list and tuple

Exercises in this lesson

  • Reversing a list — Working with lists
  • List and Tuple Toolkit — Build, slice, mutate, and test
