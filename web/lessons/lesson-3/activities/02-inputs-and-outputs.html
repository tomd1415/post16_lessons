Teacher guidance (answer prompts)

  • Quick checks: IO symbol = parallelogram; input = data/signals in; output example = heater on.
  • Expect at least 2 inputs and 2 outputs per chosen device.
  • Misconceptions: confusing “water in” (environment) with “valve on” (controller output); forgetting that a sound/light is an output.
