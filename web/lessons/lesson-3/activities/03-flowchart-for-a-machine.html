Teacher guidance (answer prompts)

  • Quick checks: start/end = oval; decision needs two exits; exit condition prevents infinite loop.
  • Look for labelled Yes/No paths and an explicit End.
  • Misconceptions: leaving outputs in environment (e.g., “water” instead of “valve on”).
