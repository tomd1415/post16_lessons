Step 4: Reflection

Where did you add a decision? Did any step need to loop? What would make the flowchart clearer?

Teacher guidance (answer prompts)

  • Quick checks: decision = diamond; sequence = ordered steps; pseudocode = structured plain language.
  • Look for a clear Yes/No labelling on the diamond; at least one loop or explicit end.
  • Misconceptions: arrows that jump backwards without condition; missing End.
